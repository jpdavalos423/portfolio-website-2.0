Worked in UCSD's Early Research Scholars Program under Professor Christine Alvarado, building data infrastructure and research tooling to study California CS transfer pathways at scale. Built a Python and Selenium scraping pipeline that extracted and structured 1,044 articulation agreements from 115 community colleges, then used that data to develop a prerequisite-aware pathway generator that outputs term-by-term course schedules. The research culminated in co-authored work submitted to SIGCSE 2026 on articulation gaps and transfer access in California CS programs.
