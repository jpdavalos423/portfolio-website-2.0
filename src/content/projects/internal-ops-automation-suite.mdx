## Overview

A collection of internal tools used to automate recurring operational workflows.

## Problem

Business operations depended on manual spreadsheets and follow-up messages across several teams.

## What I Built

An admin-facing app for workflow definitions, automated reminders, and reporting exports.

## Technical Decisions

I optimized for traceability and incremental feature rollout over broad initial scope.

## Impact

Teams saved time on coordination tasks and gained better visibility into operational status.
